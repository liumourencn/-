# Platform heuristics

## YouTube

Prioritize:
- Title-thumbnail promise: what curiosity gap or outcome is promised before click.
- First 15-30 seconds: how the video confirms the promise and previews payoff.
- Retention loops: open questions, chapter transitions, stakes escalation, pattern interrupts.
- Authority and proof: data, examples, credentials, experiments, before/after evidence.
- Long-form structure: intro, context, problem framing, chapters, climax, recap, call to action.

Common reusable formulas:
- “I tried X for Y days; here is what actually changed.”
- “Everyone says X, but the missing detail is Y.”
- “I analyzed N examples and found one repeatable pattern.”

## Douyin / 抖音

Prioritize:
- First 1-3 seconds: visual surprise, conflict, result front-loading, direct benefit.
- High-density rhythm: one idea per shot, short sentences, fast subtitles, quick scene changes.
- Comment triggers: controversial question, identity challenge, “你是不是也…”, choice prompts.
- Immediate payoff: avoid long setup; show result or conflict before explanation.
- Vertical-native cues: large captions, obvious subject, strong facial/action framing.

Common reusable formulas:
- “别再 X 了，真正有效的是 Y。”
- “我用 X 解决了 Y，过程比你想的简单。”
- “99% 的人忽略了这个细节。”

## Bilibili / B站

Prioritize:
- UP主 persona: expertise, humor, self-aware tone, community relationship.
- Knowledge density: sections, examples, references, comparisons, diagrams or screen recordings.
- Community language: jokes, 弹幕点, 梗, honest caveats, “省流版”.
- Title-cover alignment: promise should feel interesting but not overly营销.
- Completion motivation: chaptered payoff, progressive reveals, useful summary.

Common reusable formulas:
- “省流：X，但真正关键是 Y。”
- “这期我们把 X 讲透：从 A 到 B。”
- “看完你会知道：为什么 X 不是你想的那样。”

## Cross-platform adaptation

When adapting one video to another platform:
- YouTube to Douyin: compress setup, front-load result, split into a series, make each segment one punchline.
- Douyin to YouTube: add context, proof, chapter structure, examples, and retention loops.
- Bilibili to Douyin: retain strongest梗/观点, remove long铺垫, turn knowledge points into quick scenes.
- Douyin to Bilibili: add解释、案例、反例、UP主人设表达, and a sectioned summary.
